<p><p>Anion-exchange resins are polymeric materials used in water treatment and purification processes to remove negatively charged ions from solutions. The market consists of two main types: acid resins and alkali resins. Acid resins are designed to efficiently remove anions like chloride, nitrate, and sulfate, typically operating in acidic conditions. In contrast, alkali resins are tailored for applications in basic environments, effectively targeting anions such as phosphate and silicate. Both types are crucial for industrial, municipal, and agricultural water management applications.</p></p>
