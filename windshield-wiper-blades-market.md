<p><p>The windshield wiper blades market is divided into two main types: boneless wiper blades and bone wiper blades. Boneless wiper blades feature a sleek, frameless design that enhances aerodynamic performance and provides even pressure distribution across the blade, resulting in better visibility. In contrast, bone wiper blades, also known as traditional blades, utilize a metal frame that provides robustness and flexibility, ensuring effective contact with the windshield. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and vehicle compatibility, influencing their market penetration.</p></p>
