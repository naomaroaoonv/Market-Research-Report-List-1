<p><p>The switchable smart glass market encompasses three main types: toughened smart glass, laminated smart glass, and double-glazed smart glass. Toughened smart glass is heat-treated for enhanced strength and resistance to impact, making it ideal for commercial applications. Laminated smart glass consists of two or more glass layers bonded with an interlayer, providing safety and sound insulation. Double-glazed smart glass features two glass panes with an insulating layer, enhancing energy efficiency and soundproofing, catering to both residential and commercial needs.</p></p>
