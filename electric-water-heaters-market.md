<p><strong>The Electric Water Heaters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Storage</li><li>Non-storage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric water heater market is primarily segmented into storage and non-storage types. Storage water heaters, or tank heaters, maintain a reservoir of heated water for immediate use, making them suitable for households with high demand. In contrast, non-storage water heaters, commonly known as tankless or on-demand heaters, heat water directly as it flows through the unit, offering energy efficiency and unlimited hot water. This distinction caters to varying consumer needs based on usage patterns and energy preferences.</p></p>
