<p><p>Travel Management Systems (TMS) come in two primary market types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise TMS software is installed locally on a company's servers, offering greater control but requiring significant IT resources for maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based TMS solutions are hosted online, providing flexibility, scalability, and ease of access from anywhere with an internet connection. Cloud systems generally require less upfront investment and are frequently updated, making them popular among businesses seeking efficiency and cost-effectiveness in travel management.</p></p>
