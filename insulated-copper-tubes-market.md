<p><p>The insulated copper tubes market comprises two primary types: extruded copper tubes and drawn copper tubes. Extruded copper tubes are manufactured through a process where heated copper is forced through a shaped die, resulting in lightweight and uniform profiles suitable for various applications. In contrast, drawn copper tubes are produced by pulling copper through a die to achieve specific dimensions, offering higher strength and precision. Both types serve critical roles in HVAC, refrigeration, and plumbing industries due to their excellent thermal conductivity and durability.</p></p>
