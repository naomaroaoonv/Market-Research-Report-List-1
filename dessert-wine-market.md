<p><p>The dessert wine market comprises several distinct types, including Sauternes, Natural Sweet, and Mistelle. Sauternes, a Bordeaux specialty, is known for its balance of sweetness and acidity, often made from botrytized grapes. Natural Sweet wines are produced through methods that concentrate sugars, typically lacking added fermentation. Mistelle, another unique category, is a fortified wine made by adding alcohol to unfermented grape juice, resulting in a rich, sweet profile. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and occasions, enhancing the dessert wine landscape.</p></p>
