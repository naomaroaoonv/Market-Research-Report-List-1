<p><p>The hollow fiber membrane hemodialyzer market is categorized into low flux and high flux membranes. Low flux membranes are designed for patients with stable renal function, effectively removing small toxins while retaining essential proteins. In contrast, high flux membranes allow for greater permeability, facilitating the removal of larger molecules and providing improved clearance of waste products, making them suitable for patients with more severe kidney dysfunction. Both types cater to varying patient needs and treatment protocols in dialysis.</p></p>
