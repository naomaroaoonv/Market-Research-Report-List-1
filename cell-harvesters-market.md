<p><p>The cell harvesters market comprises two main types: displacement collectors and therapy collectors. Displacement collectors focus on efficiently harvesting cells from culture systems by displacing the surrounding medium, ensuring maximum yield and viability. Conversely, therapy collectors are designed for specific therapeutic applications, targeting the isolation of particular cell types for medical treatments, such as stem cell therapies or immunotherapies. Both types are integral to advancing biotechnological and clinical applications in cell biology and regenerative medicine.</p></p>
