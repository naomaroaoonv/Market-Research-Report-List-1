<p><p>The digital car keys market encompasses various technologies, primarily Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE), Near Field Communication (NFC), and Ultra-Wideband (UWB). BLE keys enable keyless entry and ignition through smartphone apps, providing convenience and range. NFC keys offer secure, close-range communication, unlocking vehicles with a simple tap. UWB keys, known for their precision and anti-spoofing features, allow users to unlock cars as they approach, enhancing security and user experience. Each technology caters to different consumer preferences and applications in smart automotive locking systems.</p></p>
