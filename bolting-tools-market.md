<p><ul><li>Mechanical</li><li>Hydraulic</li><li>Pneumatic</li><li>Electric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bolting tools market is categorized into four primary types: mechanical, hydraulic, pneumatic, and electric tools. Mechanical tools rely on manual force or simple mechanisms to tighten bolts, while hydraulic tools use fluid pressure for high-torque applications, providing increased efficiency in industrial settings. Pneumatic tools utilize compressed air for rapid fastening, making them ideal for assembly lines. Electric bolting tools offer versatility and are often preferred for precision tasks due to their adjustable settings and reduced operator fatigue.</p></p>
