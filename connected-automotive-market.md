<p><p>The Connected Automotive Market is categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Embedded**: This involves built-in connectivity features within the vehicle, enabling functions like navigation, safety systems, and infotainment without relying on external devices.</p><p>2. **Tethered**: This mode connects the vehicle to a smartphone or other device, using the latter's capabilities to enhance features such as internet access and app integration.</p><p>3. **Integrated**: This approach combines both embedded and tethered elements, offering seamless connectivity and enhanced functionality through advanced technology and real-time data sharing.</p></p>
