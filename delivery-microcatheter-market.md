<p><p>The delivery microcatheter market is categorized into single-lumen and dual-lumen microcatheters. Single-lumen microcatheters feature a single channel for guiding therapeutic agents, making them ideal for straightforward delivery applications. In contrast, dual-lumen microcatheters have two separate channels, allowing for simultaneous delivery of multiple substances or contrast agents, enhancing procedural flexibility. Both types play crucial roles in interventional procedures, catering to varying clinical needs and improving patient outcomes in areas such as cardiovascular and neurovascular interventions.</p></p>
