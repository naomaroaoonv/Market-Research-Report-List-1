<p><p>The disposable transfer pipettes market includes two primary types: graduated and nongraduated pipettes. Graduated transfer pipettes feature measurement markings along the length, allowing for precise liquid volume measurements, making them suitable for applications requiring accuracy. In contrast, nongraduated transfer pipettes lack measurement markings, offering a simpler design for general liquid transfer tasks where precision is less critical. Both types are popular in laboratories, healthcare, and research settings for their convenience, hygiene, and ease of disposal after single-use.</p></p>
