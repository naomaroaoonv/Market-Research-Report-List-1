<p><p>The roofing membranes market encompasses various types, including thermoplastic membranes, synthetic rubber (thermoset), and modified bitumen. Thermoplastic membranes, like PVC and TPO, offer flexibility and weldability, making them ideal for commercial applications. Synthetic rubber membranes, particularly EPDM, provide durability and resistance to UV exposure, suitable for diverse climates. Modified bitumen combines asphalt with polymers for enhanced performance, offering ease of installation and good waterproofing, often favored in residential roofing. Each type caters to specific needs and environmental conditions.</p></p>
