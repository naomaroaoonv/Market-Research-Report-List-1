<p><p>The neurovascular thrombectomy device market includes various types of devices aimed at treating clot-related neurological conditions. Mechanical/fragmentation thrombectomy devices disrupt clots for easier removal. Stent retrievers capture and extract clots using a mesh within the artery. Basket/brush retrievers ensnare clots with a basket-like structure. Coil retrievers deploy coils to anchor and remove clots. Aspiration thrombectomy devices use suction to extract clots. Rheolytic/hydrodynamic devices employ fluid dynamics to break up clots, while ultrasonic devices utilize sound waves for clot disintegration.</p></p>
