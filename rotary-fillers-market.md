<p><p>Rotary fillers are categorized into three market types based on their operating mechanisms: manual, semi-automatic, and automatic. Manual rotary fillers require human operators to load and operate the machine, making them suitable for low-volume production. Semi-automatic fillers combine manual loading with automated filling processes, improving efficiency for medium production levels. Automatic rotary fillers are fully automated, handling all aspects of filling without human intervention, which is ideal for large-scale operations, offering high speed and consistent accuracy.</p></p>
