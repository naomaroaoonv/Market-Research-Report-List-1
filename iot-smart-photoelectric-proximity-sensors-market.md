<p><strong>The IoT Smart Photoelectric Proximity Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Through-Beam Sensors</li><li>Retro-Reflective Sensors</li><li>Diffuse Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IoT Smart Photoelectric Proximity Sensors market primarily consists of three types: Through-Beam Sensors, Retro-Reflective Sensors, and Diffuse Sensors. Through-Beam Sensors consist of a transmitter and receiver, ensuring high detection accuracy over long distances. Retro-Reflective Sensors utilize a reflector to bounce the signal back, making them versatile for various applications with moderate range. Diffuse Sensors operate by detecting light reflected off an object, offering a compact solution for short-range sensing in diverse environments, enhancing automated processes and operational efficiency.</p></p>
