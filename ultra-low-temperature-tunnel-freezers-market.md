<p><p>Ultra Low Temperature Tunnel Freezers are specialized equipment used for rapid freezing of products, particularly in the food and pharmaceutical industries. The market is divided into two main types: Single-Channel and Multi-Channel. Single-Channel freezers feature a single freezing pathway, ideal for smaller operations or specific applications, while Multi-Channel freezers offer multiple freezing pathways, allowing for higher throughput and versatility. Multi-Channel systems are suitable for large-scale processing, maximizing efficiency and enabling simultaneous handling of diverse products.</p></p>
